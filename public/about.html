This app is designed to help writers stay motivated and track their progress by making word count tracking effortless. Whether you're working on a novel, an article, or daily journaling, the intuitive interface lets you log your writing sessions, visualize your progress with interactive charts, and set custom goals to keep yourself accountable. With features like streak tracking, daily and monthly word count insights, and ability to export to csv, you’ll always know how far you’ve come—and how much further you can go. Stay consistent, stay inspired, and reach your writing goals with ease!

This is an open source project created by Katie Yang who is trying to hit her own writing goals. Feel free to reach out to her at 13hy22@queensu.ca or raise any issues/suggestions on the GitHub repository here .
